About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you progress, you'll gain experience points to level up and unlock powerful abilities.

Each run is unique, offering different power-up combinations and challenging you to adapt your strategy. Will you focus on offensive abilities to clear monsters quickly, or defensive upgrades to enhance your survival chances?

Tips for Success

Beginner Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded
  • Prioritize experience collection early game
  • Choose complementary power-ups

Advanced Strategies

  • Create build synergies
  • Kite enemies effectively
  • Time your power-up selections
